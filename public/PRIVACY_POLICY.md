# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 18, 2026**

## Overview

Multiplication Kingdom ("we," "our," or "us") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.** 

The app stores only game progress data (stars earned, unlocked kingdoms) locally on your device using device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any servers or third parties.

## No Accounts, No Registration

- We do not require account creation
- We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiable information
- We do not track individual users

## No Analytics or Tracking

- We do not use analytics services
- We do not track your gameplay behavior
- We do not monitor how you use the app

## No Advertising

- We do not display ads
- We do not use advertising networks
- We do not collect data for marketing purposes

## No Third-Party Services

- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not use third-party SDKs that collect personal data
- We do not integrate with social media services

## Data Stored On Your Device

Game progress (kingdoms, stars, achievements) is saved locally on your device only. You can clear this data at any time through your device's app storage settings.

## Children's Privacy

Multiplication Kingdom is a child-friendly educational game with **no data collection**. It is safe for children of all ages and is COPPA compliant.
